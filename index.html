Section 3 – Challenges with Media Tools

One specific challenge I see within media consumption is the addictive nature of many platforms, as they are engineered to keep users engaged for as long as possible. I had to delete all my social media apps from my phone in order to prevent myself from wasting scores of hours each week mindlessly scrolling my life away. This is something that I believe is one of the most pressing issues for Gen Z and Gen Alpha, as this technology is preventing socialization, inducing isolation, and engendering the largest epidemic of anxiety and depression the world has ever seen.
